Minister Quote

“The Home Team plays a crucial role in keeping Singapore safe and secure. We prepare and train for many scenarios every day. But it is often only during crises that we realise how prepared we are, and how well we have trained for the situation that we are facing. The current COVID-19 situation is a timely reminder of how important training and learning are, amidst the volatile and uncertain security landscape that we operate in. As we continue to fight the virus, we must press on with our training and upskilling efforts, to ensure that our Home Team officers will continue to be resilient, agile and ready for new challenges that lie ahead.”

- Mrs Josephine Teo, Second Minister for Home Affairs
